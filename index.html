In Akita Finance, source of your yield will depend on your participation in the protocol

As a lender, your yield comes from:

  • Interest paid by borrowers to open leverage yield farming (interest rate is based on the pool utilization)
  • Incentive rewards paid in AKITA

As a yield farmer, you yield comes from:
  • Underlying trading fee of the pool - i.e., trading fee of the PancakeSwap pool
  • Incentive rewards of the pool, if applicable - e.g, Pancake token
  • Incentive rewards paid in AKITA (only if you have a leverage position)

You can participate in Akita Finance in four ways:

  • As a lender
  • As a Farmer
  • As a bounty hunter
  • As a Liquidator
